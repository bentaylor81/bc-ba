BC-BA Membership Types

Individual Membership

Personal

  • Suitable for individuals who are seeking access to all BC-BA networks, benefits and events. Includes a personal listing in the members directory. Not transferable.
  • $150.00 – Plus GST - Apply Here

Entrepreneurs

  • For privately owned business with fewer than 10 employees and/or sales of less than $1,000,000. Membership admits 3 person with full member listing. Not transferable.

Memberships includes the following benefits:

  • 3 national representatives free-entry to all networking events
  • Will be provided the opportunity, at reasonable cost, for short presentation at breakfast events
  • Will be listed in the Membership Directory
  • Active introduction to networked chambers/business associations as required
  • Preferential member benefits program
  • Option to purchase bi-monthly updated attendance lists from all networking events
  • Preferential rates for commercial exposure at annual events i.e. Christmas Lunch, Golf Tournament, breakfast/lunch meetings
  • Exclusive event sponsorship opportunities
  • $375.00 – Plus GST - Apply Here

Corporate Membership

Includes the following benefits:
  • 6 national representatives free-entry to all networking events
  • Will be provided the opportunity, at reasonable cost, for short presentation at a breakfast events
  • Will be listed in the Membership Directory
  • Active introduction to networked chambers/business associations as required
  • Preferential member benefits program
  • Bi-monthly updated attendance lists from all networking events at no charge
  • Preferential rates for commercial exposure at annual events i.e. Christmas Lunch, Golf Tournament, breakfast/lunch meetings
  • Exclusive event sponsorship opportunities
  • $750.00 – Plus GST - Apply Here

Please note: Cost of annual membership is in Canadian funds.
