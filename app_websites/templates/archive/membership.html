BC-BA Membership

The BC-BA exists solely to promote and support the development of trade, commerce and investment between UK and Canada by pro-actively connecting businesses, associations, individuals and government agencies.
  1. Actively supports and promotes the transatlantic business interests of its members.
  2. Provides a business networking forum for companies and individuals engaged in business between Canada and the UK.
  3. BCBA maintains a close working relationship with the British High Commission and its offices across Canada and provides a respected advocacy platform for members’ views.
  4. Cross-Promotion of other Chambers’ events and sharing of pertinent commercial information with BCBA members.
  5. Access to a wealth of Information. Through its various communications vehicles and direct access to the business communities and networks, your membership investment in the British Canadian Business Association opens the door to a wealth of information and business initiatives.
